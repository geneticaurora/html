About me...

>Lets get Personal.

My name is Robert Evans I am 37 years of age, I am originally from the Rhondda Valleys 'boyo' in South Wales and and have lived in Manchester since late 2012. I moved to Manchester to escape the constraints and lack of opportunities in a small village in South Wales. it's been one of roller coaster of a journey and I think I am finally finding myself at last. It's been a rollercoaster the last few years due to many unfortunate circumstances and this is my time to shine
